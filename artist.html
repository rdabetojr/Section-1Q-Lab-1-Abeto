Kamikazee

Kamikazee is a Filipino rock band known for its high-energy performances and a unique blend of pop-punk, alternative rock, and hard rock. Formed in 2000, the band is recognized for its comedic and satirical lyrics, often incorporating pop culture references. Kamikazee's live shows are known for their lively stage antics, including liberal use of expletives and suggestive movements, which, despite their wildness, don't detract from the musicality of their performances according to Tower of Doom.
